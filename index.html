What is Sound Ninja?


SoundNinja, in its debut iteration, is a chrome extension designed to give you immediate access to the artists you read about. In any web page you visit, the artists’ names will be identified in text and you will be given access to a preview of their most recent work.
Simply hover your mouse over the artist name to activate the preview, if you like what you hear we’ve also given you a direct link to their artist page in the iTunes store! No need to interrupt your reading with an artist search in a new tab, save time and streamline your browsing with this free app.


-The Ninja
